Guide
HOW TO TAKE YOUR MEASUREMENTS
👙 Bust / Chest
Measure around the fullest part of your chest, tape parallel to floor.
⬛ Waist
Measure around your natural waist — narrowest part above belly button.
🔵 Hips
Measure around the fullest part of your hips, about 8 inches below waist.
📏 Body Length
Measure from top of shoulder down to where you want the outfit to end.

Tips for Accurate Measurements

  • Use a flexible measuring tape — not a ruler
  • Stand straight with arms at your sides
  • Do not pull the tape too tight
  • Have someone help you for better accuracy
